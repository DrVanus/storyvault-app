Quick fixes

🔄
Stuck on "Continuing the story…"?

The AI request takes 5–15 seconds per turn (longer for the per-turn illustration). If it sits there for over 60 seconds, force-quit the app and tap the same choice again — your progress is saved.

🪙
Restored Pro purchase?

Profile → Upgrade → Restore Purchases. If your Apple ID has an active StoryVault Pro subscription or Lifetime, it'll re-link in seconds.

🪶
Hit the free-tier cap?

Free users get 3 AI Adventure turns per calendar month. The counter resets on the 1st of each month, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited.

📖
Reader text size or theme?

Inside any chapter, tap the Aa icon in the top-right toolbar. Adjustments persist across all books.

🐛
Found a bug or weird AI output?

Email hi@storyvault.app with what you saw and which scenario you were in. Screenshots help.

💸
Refund or billing question?

All purchases are handled by Apple — request refunds via reportaproblem.apple.com. We can't process refunds directly.

For reviewers, journalists, teachers

If you're evaluating StoryVault AI for a publication, classroom, or library — or want a Pro review code — drop a line at hi@storyvault.app. The bundled library is all public-domain and the AI Adventure system prompt explicitly excludes explicit sexual content and child endangerment, so it's safe for 12+ classroom use.

Where the data goes

The full data flow is in our privacy policy — short version: your story input goes to OpenAI for generation and isn't stored, your saved adventures live on your device, no login, no ads, no tracking.
