Common questions

FAQ

Everything reviewers, readers, and parents ask us.

Is the AI just rewriting the original book?

No — the AI uses the book's world and voice as the setting, but every adventure is its own continuity. You're not reading Dracula. You're at Castle Dracula, making choices Jonathan Harker never had.

What if the AI says something off?

It happens. Tap "Try again" on any turn to regenerate, or type a different choice to steer the story. The system prompt is tuned to keep prose in-period and on-tone, but AI is AI.

Does the AI train on my stories?

No. Your input is sent to our backend for the duration of the request, forwarded to OpenAI for generation, and not stored on either end. We don't sell or share your input.

Can I read offline?

The 87 bundled books are fully offline-readable. AI Adventure requires a network connection — every turn is generated live by the model.

What happens if I cancel Pro?

You keep access through the end of the current period, then drop back to the free tier (3 AI Adventure turns / month). Your saved adventures and library stay — they're on your device.

How is this different from AI Dungeon?

StoryVault is built around literature — period-correct prose, hand-tuned scenarios, a real reader for the source books. The AI keeps you in the source's voice instead of letting the story drift into generic fantasy.

iPad? Mac? Android?

iOS first. iPad works on the same iOS app. Android and Mac are on the roadmap but not committed yet.

I'm a teacher / parent / reviewer — how do I evaluate it?

The bundled library is all public-domain material rated 12+. The AI Adventure system prompt explicitly excludes explicit sexual content and child endangerment. Reach out at dmusk188@gmail.com for review codes.
